O. Giroud

Giroud is a soccer player. He plays for Chelsea in the English Premier League. his nationality is French. He plays with very decent soccer players on his national team. His fellow teammates consist of Greizmann, Pogba, Kante, and best of all M'bappe. These five players have single handedly carried their team through the the 2018 World Cup finale.
